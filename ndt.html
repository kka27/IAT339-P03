For my IAT235: Information Design class, we created a microsite for the event Now here, Now always, our goal is to captivate prospective audience members and provide them with a clear understanding of the performance's theme: the essence of being human. Our microsite targets individuals familiar with contemporary dance theatre, particularly fans of Nederlands Dans Theater.

The microsite design emphasizes visual engagement while minimizing reliance on micro interactions. It offers concise descriptions of the show, detailing what audiences can expect from each act of Now here, Now always, which spans three play periods. Users can easily navigate to book tickets and access program information.

The choice of visual elements, including the use of Spectral and Source Sans typefaces, is deliberate. Spectral's elegant triangular serifs and moderate contrast convey a sense of contemporary sophistication, aligning with the essence of Now here, Now always. Meanwhile, Source Sans complements Spectral's aesthetic with its balanced stroke contrast and similar x-heights, ensuring a cohesive and visually appealing design.

By strategically incorporating these design elements, our microsite effectively communicates the complexities of humanity to frequent dance audiences, achieving our objective with minimal content.
